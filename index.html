Irresponsible Drinking

Alcohol abuse is very common around the world and traces are present where they shouln't be. Abuse of alcohol can lead irrational judgement and movement. This has led to many hospitalisations due to injuries and unfortunate deaths around the world.

Incidents Related to Alcohol consumption in Australia

Australia has a well-known drinking culture. As the drinking minimum age is 18, most drinkers start from a very young age. A large portion of the Australian population drinks and abuse of alcohol, especially among the younger generations is common.

Since a large population of Australia drink alcohol, alcohol caused hospitalisations are common for a various number of injuries and some hospitalisations lead to deaths. Often, in cases of injuries, there are also illicit drugs present in the patient.
